About Veagle

The Eyes of
Football Intelligence

Veagle is an AI-powered football analytics platform built on cutting-edge computer vision and deep learning. We process raw match video to automatically detect, track, and analyze every element on the pitch — so you spend less time watching footage and more time making decisions.

From professional clubs to youth academies, Veagle provides the analytical depth once reserved for elite teams — now accessible to any organization ready to compete at the next level.

Core Capabilities

Everything Your Team
Needs to Win

Player Detection & Tracking

YOLO-powered object detection combined with ByteTrack and DeepSORT for frame-perfect player tracking across the entire pitch.

  • Multi-class detection (players, refs, ball)
  • Player re-identification across cuts
  • Sub-pixel tracking accuracy

Heatmaps & Movement

Colour-coded movement density maps that reveal positional patterns, high-press zones, and defensive shape across every 90 minutes.

  • Individual & team heatmaps
  • Zone occupation analysis
  • Time-segmented HM slices

Speed & Distance Analytics

Real-time physical performance metrics extracted directly from video — no wearables required. Track sprints, distance covered, and intensity zones.

  • Top-speed detection per player
  • Distance covered per phase
  • High-intensity run counts

Pass Detection & Networks

Automated pass event detection with directional vectors, success rates, and interactive pass-network visualizations per match period.

  • Pass map generation
  • Completion rate by player
  • Build-up pattern analysis

Possession Analysis

Frame-by-frame ball possession attribution with zone-based breakdowns, pressure events, and territorial control metrics.

  • Team & half-court possession
  • Possession under pressure
  • Transition speed metrics

Tactical & Event Reports

Automated detection of key events — shots, fouls, corners, set-pieces — compiled into structured, shareable PDF or dashboard reports.

  • Shot map & xG estimates
  • Set-piece detection
  • One-click report export
